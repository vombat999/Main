ABOUT

G-CARD

G-CARD extension is an environment for customization and editing of product cards for Shopify. Designed to simplify the use and administration of an online store on the Shopify platform, the extension is responsible for the following tasks: editing the product card; displaying data inside the product card, dividing into languages, adding main and subsequent photos; displaying information in sections.
